About This Dashboard

Built by Oussama Khoulaidi, Cybersecurity & Systems Engineer, as a GRC portfolio project demonstrating hands-on knowledge of compliance tools and workflows used by a Security Compliance Officer.

The fictional company DataFlow Qatar provides a realistic scenario covering ISO 27001, PDPPL, PCI-DSS, vendor risk management, and KPI tracking.

📍 Doha, Qatar  ·  📧 oussamakhoulaidi@gmail.com  ·  LinkedIn: oussama-khoulaidi

Frameworks Covered

  • ISO/IEC 27001:2022 — ISMS, Annex A (93 controls), SoA, internal audit
  • Qatar PDPPL — Law No. 13 of 2016, consent, 72-hr breach notification
  • PCI-DSS v4.0 — 12 requirements, CDE scoping, QSA audit
  • Qatar NIA Framework — National Information Assurance baseline
  • Kuwait CITRA — Cybersecurity Regulations (Resolution 26/2024)

GRC Concepts Demonstrated

  • 5×5 risk heat map — inherent vs. residual risk movement
  • RAG (Red/Amber/Green) compliance status tracking with filters
  • Corrective action management and overdue tracking
  • 12-month KPI trend monitoring
  • Vendor risk scoring and tiering (Critical/High/Medium/Low)
  • Multi-framework compliance coverage view
